# What's New in Tensies 🎲

A friendly log of everything that's changed. Newest stuff up top.

## Saturday, May 30, 2026 ⏸️

- **Hosts can pause the game now.** Snack break? Doorbell? "Wait, who's actually
  winning?" Hit pause and the table waits politely for everyone.
- Paused games are patient — they'll hang around for up to an hour, so putting
  your phone down mid-match no longer means the game gives up on you.
- Your seat at the table is now locked to you. If your connection hiccups, you
  come back to *your* spot — nobody can wander into it while you're gone.
- _Behind the scenes: we're getting smarter about how every game plays out, so we
  can keep making matches more fun, fair, and engaging — and make sure new features
  like pause just work._

## Friday, May 29, 2026

- Added a menu button to the game — a tidy little home for new options (pause was
  the first to move in).
- Switching between screens feels smoother and skips a needless flicker.
- _Behind the scenes: we're sharpening our understanding of how games unfold so we
  can keep making them more fun, fair, and engaging._

## Thursday, May 28, 2026

- A friendlier waiting screen now greets you when things are loading or
  reconnecting — no more jarring pop-up dialogs.
- Your dice stay exactly where you left them, even after a refresh or a surprise
  disconnect. No more shuffling when you blink.
- Squashed a bug where the winner banner would overstay its welcome and refuse to
  leave.
- Fixed dice clumping together awkwardly when a new game or reconnect kicked in.
- _Behind the scenes: we're tuning the game's foundations so everything stays fast,
  fair, and ready for the fun stuff coming next._

## Wednesday, May 27, 2026

- Drop off and you can now jump right back in — there's a short grace period and a
  clear "reconnecting…" screen so a flaky phone signal doesn't end your game.
- Made the dice roll animation buttery smooth and fixed a sneaky freeze when a
  re-roll landed on the exact same numbers. Spooky, but no longer sticky.
- Tensies got a face! New dice logo, a proper favicon, and a nice preview image
  when you share a game link.
- Join errors now show up on the right screen instead of yelling into the void.

## Tuesday, May 26, 2026

- Dice rolls are now refereed by the server, so every roll is fair and square —
  no funny business.

## Monday, May 25, 2026 ✨

- **Invite friends in a tap.** Share a join link or fire off a text — getting a
  game going is now the easy part.
- Joining a game got its own clean screen, complete with a randomly suggested name
  in case inspiration runs dry.
- The dice came alive: smoother rolls, nicer shadows, and dice that politely keep
  their distance instead of piling on top of each other.
- Polished the look all around — a bolder round label and a roll button that sits
  right on the wooden table and gives a satisfying push when you tap it.
- Made it play nicely on iPhones: no accidental zooming, no jumpy scrolling, and
  rapid-fire taps still roll just fine.
