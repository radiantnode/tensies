# What's New in Tensies

Pull up a stool. Newest stuff up top.

## 1.5.0 ("Honest Pour")

Thursday, June 18, 2026

- Nobody can cook the dice anymore. Every roll now draws from a public randomness beacon, so a win is a win and not a thumb on the scale. 🎲
- After a game you can pull up the results and see for yourself that the rolls were on the level. Look for the Roll Trust shield.
- There's a new post-game page that walks through how a match actually played out, who rolled what, all of it.
- Profiles picked up some personality. Add a short bio, drop your location, and show off any badges next to your name.
- _Behind the scenes: we put real work into making every roll something you can check yourself instead of just taking our word for it._

## 1.4.0 ("Closing Time")

Wednesday, June 17, 2026

- You can actually end a game now. When the night winds down, wrap it up clean instead of everyone slowly drifting off. 🏁
- The final screen is a proper scoreboard: the faces, the scores, and whoever walked away with it. It sticks around even if you refresh.

## 1.3.1 ("Suggestion Box")

Tuesday, June 16, 2026

- _Behind the scenes: we took a hard outside look at the game and wrote down what to sharpen next, so the good stuff keeps coming._

## 1.3.0 ("Bragging Rights")

Monday, June 15, 2026

- Everybody gets a profile now. Your wins and your recent games, all on one page with your avatar up top. Tap a name to go have a look. 🏅
- Your recent games show who you played and how it went, win or loss.
- Signing in got smoother, and a busted join link finally works again.
- _Behind the scenes: we straightened out how wins and games get counted so your record matches the night you actually had._

## 1.2.0 ("House Account")

Sunday, June 14, 2026

- Make yourself an account with a passkey. No password to forget, and your stats come along for the ride. 🔑
- Once you're signed in, the app knows you by name and tucks the name box away.
- Fixed the game so it runs right to the edges on an iPhone, instead of leaving an awkward gap behind the home bar.

## 1.1.0 ("Top Shelf")

Saturday, June 13, 2026

- The dice on the front give a little wiggle now, and the buttons catch a shine when you glance at them. ✨
- Tidied up the lobby. Share and Play sit side by side, and you'll see both your YOU and HOST badges, with you bumped to the top of the list.
- _Behind the scenes: we built a little workbench for tuning the share-by-sound trick so it lands clean in a noisy room._

## 1.0.0 ("Opening Tab")

Friday, June 12, 2026

- A bar regular and his crew love playing Tensies, the dice game. One night, a few heated rounds deep, he figured it'd be great to play anywhere, even when you forget the actual dice. So he started having Claude build it, sketched the very first board himself, and has kept tinkering on it from his barstool between rounds. 🍺
- Round up the gang in a tap. Fire off a text or use the share sheet right from the lobby.
- Out of signal, or just feeling fancy? Hit Play to chirp the game code across the bar and have a friend hit Listen to grab it out of the air.
- Turn your phone sideways and we'll gently point you back to portrait, where Tensies lives.
- _Behind the scenes: the whole thing only starts at "version one" because he forgot to run git init until the game already worked. Classic._
