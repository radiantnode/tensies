## Findings

- **Step 27: the known 2026-06-08 post-reveal `showFor()` race fired (759ms Winner overlay, prod R3).** Mechanism: Beta's fast roll broadcast (270ms cadence) landed after Alpha's reveal completed but while the overlay was open; `handleMessage` routed it to `showFor()` → unconditional `hideWinner()`. This is the **documented, pre-existing** bug from the 2026-06-08 logs ("Still unfixed in code — watch for it again, especially when Beta rolls fast"), observed at 1153ms then, 759ms now. **Not a rewrite regression** — the rewrite ports `showFor`'s behavior verbatim by contract; the postRevealState flash fix (2026-06-07) is intact (Step 22: 12/12 overlays ≥3003ms). The fix remains the known TODO: guard `handleMessage` to stash same-round `state` frames while `#winner-overlay` is open instead of routing to `showFor()`.
